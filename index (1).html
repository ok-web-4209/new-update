Common Questions

Answers before you ever pick up the phone

Still unsure? Request a free consultation or call (954) 459-4236.

Is the consultation really free?+
Yes. Every initial consultation with Hoffman Legal is 100% free and confidential, with no obligation to hire us. Call (954) 459-4236 or submit the contact form and we will get back to you quickly.
How quickly will someone get back to me?+
Attorney David Hoffman personally returns calls 24/7, including nights and weekends. Legal emergencies do not wait for business hours, and neither do we.
What areas of Florida do you serve?+
Hoffman Legal is based in Dania Beach and primarily serves Broward, Miami-Dade, and Palm Beach counties. Representation is available throughout Florida depending on the matter.
What should I do right after an arrest or accident?+
Stay calm and do not discuss the facts with police, insurance adjusters, or anyone other than a lawyer. Call an attorney as soon as possible. In an accident, document the scene and get medical attention even if you feel fine.
Do I need a lawyer for a minor offense?+
Even a misdemeanor can affect your permanent record, employment, professional licenses, driving privileges, or immigration status. A short consultation will make clear what is actually at stake before you decide.
Will my conversation be confidential?+
Yes. Attorney-client privilege protects what you share with David during a consultation. Simply submitting the website contact form does not by itself create the attorney-client relationship.
What should I bring to my consultation?+
Bring any paperwork you have received — citation, arrest report, court notice, accident report, insurance correspondence — along with a list of your questions. David can start with what you remember.

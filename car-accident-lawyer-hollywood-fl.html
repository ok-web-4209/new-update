What to Do After a Car Accident in Hollywood

  • Call the police and get a crash report. An official report is important documentation for any later claim.
  • Get medical care within 14 days. Florida PIP requires prompt treatment, and early records connect your injuries to the crash.
  • Document everything — photos of the scene and vehicles, the other driver's information, and the names of any witnesses.
  • Do not give a recorded statement to the other driver's insurer or accept a quick settlement before speaking with a lawyer.
  • Keep records of medical visits, time missed from work, and out-of-pocket costs related to the crash.
